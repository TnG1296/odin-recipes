Mozzarella Pizza!

Description

Pizza is great because its really easy to make and it tastes delicious

A classic dish loved by almost everyone, pizza tops the list of the most popular foods in the world. Every year, people consume around 5 billion pizzas

    Ingredients
  • Easy no knead pizza dough (or your fav pizza dough)
  • Fresh or low-moisture mozzarella
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh basil, garlic, chili flakes (optional)
    Steps
  1. Start by making this 1-bowl dough
  2. When the dough is ready, stretch it into a large rectangular shape.
  3. Top with mozzarella, a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper
  4. Bake until golden and deliciously crispy.
  5. Drizzle again with extra virgin olive oil, and enjoy!
